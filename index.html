Minesweeper

Minesweeper is a very similar game to the original game with the same name. It was created to fill the gap after the original's removal from modern Windows releases.

The objective is to carefully clear all the mines on a rectangular board, without detonating them.

  • Language: C#
  • Technologies: .NET, GTK2
  • Operating Systems: Windows, Mac, Linux

MemoryBlocks

MemoryBlocks is a tile-matching puzzle game that consists of an array of randomly dealt tiles with their pictures side down.
Tiles can be fipped over by clicking on any two of them. If the tiles match (they have identical pictures), they will be removed.
The goal is to clear the table by matching tiles with the same picture, two by two.

  • Language: C#
  • Technologies: .NET, GTK2
  • Operating Systems: Windows, Mac, Linux

eRepublik Stats Tools

This is a collection of tools that I used to create player statistics for a browser game that I used to play, called eRepublik.
It inclues a tool that scans all the players of a military unit each week, and generates a BBCode article with their progress and activity for the past week, a top 10 and awards for the best of them

Another tool is an IRC bot that can be given a wide range of commands, like mass messages for a group of players, or even a lookup and lite statistics about them. It was used on many eRepublik-related channels but I took it down due to lack of a 24/7 host at the time.

There is also a tool that used to generate wiki pages for players that included all the info that can be gathered from their public profile and even a short history, in a very nice looking and orderly format, with custom templates and multi-language support.

  • Language: C#
  • Technologies: .NET, WinForms
  • Operating Systems: Windows
